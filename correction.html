About Us


Aero Club Nitte is a technological club based in NMAMIT, Nitte. Sailing into its eighth year, the club has excelled in various technical domains. Flying high like a falcon it has spread its wings in the field of Avionics, Aerodynamics, Robotics, Internet Vision Technology and Software Development.

Our Vision

To create dynamic and exciting campus life environment that builds community, introduces students to new technical experiences as well as challenges and supports students to become active global citizens.


Our Mission

To encourage students to execute their innovative ideas through technical clubs, by creating an ecosystem which will be functional round the clock and managed fully by students under the mentorship of both internal and external resource persons.
